**Description**: An interactive dashboard analyzing healthcare claims to identify trends and optimize reimbursement processes.

**Technologies Used**: Power BI, Python, Excel

**Key Features**:
- Trend analysis of claim submissions over time.
- Identification of top claim categories and their denial rates.
- Insights into reimbursement timelines.

**Visuals**:
![Healthcare Claims Dashboard](images/healthcare_claims_dashboard.png)

**Usage**: Download the `healthcare.pbix` file in Power BI to explore the dashboard.
